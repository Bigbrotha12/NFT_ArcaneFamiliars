Explore the Familiar planes, discover the secrets of the ancients, and experiments with new magical recipes.

A wizard stole a powerful gem from a newly discovered ruin and then vanished. In this single act, he sparked the beginning of a war between the different planes: The Aelian, the Sylvan, the Mercurial, the Infernal, and the Ethereans.

You must choose which plane your wizard will help defend against the other planes.

Will you aid Charis as it battles with Aelian, gather knowledge before Nerandas itself is no more, or delve deep into Infernal in search of the gem to save your home.
